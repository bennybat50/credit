Our commitment

  • Since it directly benefits the communities where we serve, work, and live, expanding mutually beneficial relationships with certified minority, women, LGBTQ, veteran, and small-business suppliers is important to us.
  • By valuing diversity, we may increase the number of vendors we work with and find more high-quality products and services that satisfy our demands at reasonable, attractive prices.
  • Our dedication to inclusive procurement procedures that generate shared value with a broad collection of suppliers reflects our belief that we can achieve more together.

Qualifying suppliers

The Supplier Diversity Program at Harpcredit offers qualified small and diverse companies the chance to become one of our authorized suppliers of goods or services.

The following suppliers are eligible for this program, but not just these:

  • Small Disadvantaged Businesses (SDBs)
  • Minority Business Enterprises (MBEs)
  • Women Business Enterprises (WBEs)
  • Veteran Business Enterprises (VBEs)
  • Lesbian, Gay, Bi-Sexual, Transgender Business Enterprises (LGBTBEs)
  • Disability Owned Business Enterprises (DOBEs)
