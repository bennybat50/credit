We also inform our staff members and clients about the significant part they can play in the formulation of public policy.

WHY DO WE NOT VOTE POLITICALLY?

It's surprising to see how politically divided the American people are. On any given day, one can find experts discussing a wide range of political, economic, and social issues on television, in magazines, in newspapers, or online. The media itself is becoming more and more concerned with attracting readers or viewers by supporting one side of the argument over another, or by focusing on drama and conflict at the expense of properly disseminating a variety of viewpoints. As a result, discussions on these subjects are frequently reduced to a single set of words that contradict previous statements, leaving little room for compromise or agreement.
