ENHANCED BY DIVERSITY

HarpCredit was established on the principle that more Main Street residents should have access to the same advantages as Wall Street in the investment industry. Since then, that inclusiveness has served as our compass. We are dedicated to creating and upholding a culture that values and reflects the potential of all work. We are aware that diversity allows us to access a wide range of perspectives and knowledge, which supports our corporate strategy at Through Clients' Eyes.

EMPLOYEE DIVERSITY

We put a lot of effort into attracting a diverse group of talented individuals by upholding a strong employer brand and broadening where and how we connect with prospective employees. We hire members of underrepresented groups, such as women, people of color, those with disabilities, and those with a history in the military.

TOLERANT WORKPLACE

Our company's mission is to assist clients in managing their financial future, and the same is true of our employees. By creating a supportive, collaborative, adaptable, and balanced culture, we make investments in our employees and their future.

WE HAVE CREATED A SYSTEM WITH FOUR MAJOR PILLARS TO MAKE THESE IDEAS A REALITY

  • INCREASE STAFF DIVERSITY Talent stems from a variety of human traits and skills.
  • SERVING A RANGE OF MARKET NEEDS Requires utilizing our diversity, which is essential for the success of our customers.
  • EMBRACING AN INCLUSIVE CULTURE Across the entire organization will help us to improve our inclusive workplace.
  • SUPPORT OUR COMMUNITIES To have a positive effect on the communities where we live and work, we must support them.
  • CHANCES TO LEARN AND DEVELOP In order to cover the cost of approved business-related education and training, we nudge our employees to put their money into long-term professional development. And so, in order to assist people in obtaining on-the-job training and support, we provide unofficial advice from managers and partners.

CLIENTS

REPRESENTATION OF CLIENTS

We are aware that changing policy presents a significant opportunity for improvement. The HarpCredit Office of Legal and Administrative Affairs encourages the creation of ethical regulations aimed at assisting people in realizing their financial aspirations.

We train legislators, regulators, and senior branch officials at the international, state, regional, and local levels in our capacity as facilitators. On proposed legal and policy changes affecting investors, we offer our well-informed opinion. We also inform our staff members and clients about the significant part they can play in the formulation of public policy.

POLITICAL

WHY DO WE NOT VOTE POLITICALLY?

It's surprising to see how politically divided the American people are. On any given day, one can find experts discussing a wide range of political, economic, and social issues on television, in magazines, in newspapers, or online. The media itself is becoming more and more concerned with attracting readers or viewers by supporting one side of the argument over another, or by focusing on drama and conflict at the expense of properly disseminating a variety of viewpoints. As a result, discussions on these subjects are frequently reduced to a single set of words that contradict previous statements, leaving little room for compromise or agreement. Although the story is frustrating, it has been going on for a while, and it will probably end at any moment.

BELIEF

OBTAINING YOUR BELIEF

Our choices significantly affect the financial services sector as a whole. Enhancing our integrity and legitimacy is the foundation for the trust we earn from the community and from our clients. We are dedicated to upholding the highest ethical standards.

We take seriously our obligation to our shareholders. Each general voting share of HarpCredit entitles the holder to one vote at shareholders' meetings. Voting options for shareholders include mail, phone, online, and in-person.

Regionally and nationally, people have taken notice of our distinctive culture and methodology.
